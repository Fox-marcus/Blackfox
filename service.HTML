Diploma in Information technology

Details:This programme has been designed to produce graduates who will satisfy the needs of employers as well as self-employment in the 21st century and are ready to enter information and communication technology employment and are equipped to continue learning throughout their life.
The graduates from this programme will be flexible and able to meet information and communication technology challenges of the job market or industry. The programme also addresses national needs articulated through various Higher and Technical Education Policies.
It is, therefore, expected that the graduates from this programme will have aspirations to become professional technician. As such they will likely take roles involving high level managerial and organisational responsibility and be expected to provide leadership over a broad range of engineering activities. Skills required for such roles will be acquired from Management and Entrepreneurship subjects. The programme also contains coherent core of mathematics, sciences, economics and communication skills which provide a viable foundation for further studies and lifelong learning. .

Qualifications: Possession of Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (CSEE) with a pass in at least four (4) non-Religious subjects,   two   of which   must be Mathematics and English.
Possession  of  Certificate  of  Secondary  Education  Examination  (CSEE)  from  Technical Secondary  Schools with a pass in at least four (4) non-Religious subjects, two of which must be Mathematics and English and any two of the following subjects: Engineering Science/Chemistry, Architectural Drafting/Building Construction, Electrical  Engineering Science/Electrical Drafting, Workshop Technology/Mechanical Drafting.
OR
Possession  of Certificate of Secondary  Education  Examination (CSEE) with a pass in at least four (4)  non-Religious  subjects,  two  must  be  among  the  following  subjects: Mathematics ,Physics, Chemistry, English   with possession  of TRADE TEST GRADE  I / NVA LEVEL III issued by VETA. .

Bachelor of Science in Information technology

Details: This programme has been designed to produce graduates who will satisfy the needs of employers as well as self-employment in the 21st century and are ready to enter information technology employment and are equipped to continue learning throughout their life.

The graduates from this programme will be flexible and able to meet information technology challenges of the job market or industry. The programme also addresses national needs articulated through various Higher and Technical Education Policies.

It is, therefore, expected that the graduates from this programme will have aspirations to become professional technologist. As such they will likely take roles involving high level managerial and organisational responsibility and be expected to provide leadership over a broad range of information technology activities. Skills required for such roles will be acquired from Management and Entrepreneurship subjects. The programme also contains coherent core of mathematics, sciences, economics and communication skills which provide a viable foundation for further studies and lifelong learning..

Qualifications: Two Principal passes in the following subjects: Physics, Advanced Mathematics, Geography, Biology, Chemistry and Economics. Also must have a Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (CSEE) with a minimum pass of “D” grade in at least four (4) non-Religious subjects,   two   of which   must be Mathematics and English
OR
